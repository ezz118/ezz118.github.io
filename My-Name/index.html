My Name

  • 2025-07-04

My name mài jùnshēn is romanized through pinyin, while it is pronouced /mɐk̚˨ t͡sɵn˧ sɐn˥/ (jyutping: mak6 zoen3 san1) in my first language, Cantonese.

Just call me Jason or Jason Mak if you fail to pronouce them. It is a similar sound in English :)

I use ezz118 as my username throughout almost all platforms. It is originated from ezoixx118, an abbreviation for èrzhōng OI xìnxī 118 (meaning “Guangzhou No.2 High School Olympiad in Informatics player no. 118”), which was assigned from the school team when I was at primary school. I keep using it because 118 is my lucky number. I shortened it and added an ‘z’ because this letter looks “cool”.
