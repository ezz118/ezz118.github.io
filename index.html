About my name

I am a year 3 student at the University of Hong Kong. I major in Statistics and minor in Computer Science and Mathematics. I am going to apply for a research based Master or a PhD program in Statistics for 2026 fall, primarily specialized in Bayesian Inference and applications in Geographic data.

My research interest focuses on Bayesian posterior inference, including MCMC, Variational Inference and some novel learning methods, such as Normalising Flow and Diffusion Model, as well as their applications in phylogenetics, geography and linguistics. I am also interested in Gaussian Process and Spatial-temporal Statistics.

I am currently a part-time research assistant supervised by Prof. Edwin Fong, researching on Bayesian optimization. Meanwhile, I am working remotely with Prof. Cheng Li, Department of Statistics and Data Science, NUS on Gaussian Process on spatial data.

Besides Statistics, I am an enthusiast in Cartography, Toponymy, Historical Chinese Phonology (音韻學) and public transit. I post articles on Zhihu (知乎) (in Chinese only, unfortunately). I also love travelling, hiking and cycling.
